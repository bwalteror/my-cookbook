About This Cookbook

A Tribute to Fern Walter

This digital cookbook is a loving tribute to my mother, Fern. She was an amazing cook whose greatest joy was bringing family and friends together around the dinner table. Her original recipe box, filled with handwritten cards stained with vanilla and butter, is the inspiration for this project. Each recipe here holds a memory, a story, and a whole lot of love.

I built this collection to preserve her legacy and make it easy for our family, and now you, to recreate the dishes that defined our celebrations and comforted our quiet evenings.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Can I share these recipes?
A: Absolutely! Please feel free to share the link to this website with friends and family.

Q: Where are missing recipes?
A: If you have a recipe from Fern that is not listed, please email me a picture of the recipe card and send it to my email with a subject line of "Missing Recipe of Fern's" and I'll add it right away.
